# HR Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

The **HR Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project designed to provide valuable insights into workforce performance and employee attrition. The dashboard helps HR professionals and business leaders monitor key HR metrics, identify attrition trends, and make informed, data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze employee attrition across departments.
- Monitor workforce demographics.
- Identify high-risk departments.
- Understand salary-wise attrition.
- Analyze employee distribution by age group.
- Track workforce experience and average age.
- Support strategic HR decision-making.

---

## 📂 Dataset Features

The dataset contains information about employees, including:

- Employee ID
- Department
- Job Role
- Gender
- Age Group
- Salary Slab
- Total Experience
- Attrition Status
- Employee Count

## 📊 Dashboard KPIs

- 👥 Total Employees
- ✅ Active Employees
- ❌ Attrition Count
- 📉 Attrition Rate
- 🎂 Average Age
- ⭐ Average Experience

---

## 📈 Dashboard Visualizations

- Donut Chart – Attrition by Department
- Clustered Bar Chart – Attrition by Salary Slab
- Matrix – Job Role & Job Satisfaction
- Pie Chart – Attrition by Gender
- Line Chart – Attrition by Experience
- Funnel Chart – Department-wise Employee Count
- Column Chart – Employee Distribution by Age Group

---

## 🎛 Interactive Filters

- Department
- Age Group

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Administration has the highest employee count.
- Employees in lower salary slabs show higher attrition.
- Employees aged **26–35** represent the largest workforce segment.
- Male employees account for a larger proportion of attrition.
- Attrition generally decreases with increasing years of experience.
